#+TITLE: Member Cost Per Frame

How much will it cost a MakeHaven member to build a frame?

* Fixed Costs

- Bridgeport Mill Training (if not taken already) =$150=

* Per Frame

- Tubeset =$100-200=
  - BB Shell $6
  - HT $10
  - ST $15-40
  - DT $15-40
  - TT $15-30
  - SS $15-30
  - CS $20-40
- Dropouts =$10-100=
- Braze-ons, disc tab, bosses, etc =$10=
- Spray paint or professional powdercoat =$30= or =$100=
- Ream/face/chase at BSBC =$10=
