#+TITLE: Disc Brake Fixture

[[./disc-brake-fixture.png]]

Positions disc brake caliper mounts using any popular standard (IS, post mount, flat mount) and their variations.

Clamps around the dummy axle.

There's an arm for every standard that slots into the main body with precision dowel pins.

* Standards

- IS
  - IS160F
  - IS160R
- Post Mount
  - PM140
  - PM160
  - PM180
  - PM203
- Flat Mount
  - FM140F
  - FM140R
  - FM160R
  - FM180R

* Materials
