#+TITLE: Disc Brake Fixture

[[./disc-brake-fixture.png]]

Positions disc brake caliper mounts using any popular standard (IS, post mount, flat mount) and their variations.

Clamps around the dummy axle.

There's an arm for every standard that slots into the main body with precision dowel pins.

* Standards

- IS
  - IS160F
  - IS160R
- Post Mount
  - PM140
  - PM160
  - PM180
  - PM200
  - PM203
- Flat Mount
  - FM140F
  - FM140R
  - FM160R
  - FM180R

* Materials

- 0.5" 6061 aluminum plate, 5x5" =$10= (if purchased in larger piece)
- 303 stainless
  - Requirements
    - PM arm: 140x22x12
    - Base arms: 100x12x12 (3x)
    - FMR attachment: 50x30x15
    - FMF attachment: 80x33x15
    - IS attachment: 70x35x12
  - Stock
    - 5/8" plate, 160x120 bounding box (see screenshot) =$60=
- 1.375" x 7" brass round bar =$30=
  - Actual length of bushings: ~32mm each. Put entire bar in collet and use parting tool between them (5mm) -> 37mm each, plus extra for last one (30mm). Then a bit extra for the knob. Total: 30 + 37*4 = 178mm => 7"
- 4mm x 18mm stainless dowel pins 10x =$10.12= (bag of 25x)
  - https://www.mcmaster.com/91585A455/

  - M5 x 25mm SHCS, 2x (FMR to mount, sized to 15mm-thick Paragon bosses) =$2=
  - M5 x 30mm SHCS, 2x (FMF to mount) =$2=
  - M6 x 15mm SHCS, 2x (IS to mount) =$2=
