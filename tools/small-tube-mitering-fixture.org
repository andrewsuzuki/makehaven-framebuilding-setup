#+TITLE: Small-Tube Mitering Fixture

[[./small-tube-mitering-fixture.png]]

Fixtures small, possibly-tapered tubes for mitering in a Bridgeport. These could be seatstays, brake bridges, or rack tubing.

The width is 3", making it easy to pack the other side of the vise with a 1-2-3 block.

Diameter range: 9.5mm to 19mm (.375" to .75")

* Materials

- 6061 aluminum rectangular bar
  - 1.5x2x3" => 9 in3
  - 1x1.5x1.5" => 2.25 in3
  - => =~$10=
- .5" 303 stainless round bar, 3" =$2=
- M5 x ~40mm SHCS x2, =$2=
