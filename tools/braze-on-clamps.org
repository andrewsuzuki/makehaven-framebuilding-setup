#+TITLE: Braze-on Clamps

[[./braze-on-clamps.png]]

(4 count)

* Materials

- 0.5" 6061 aluminum plate. 8x5*0.5 = 20 in3, so about =$15=
- M8 x 90mm fully-threaded bolts x 4, about =$10=

** Cost

$25

* Fabrication

Waterjet, then drill and tap hole in top for bolt.

Two clamps can fit in a 4x5" piece of plate, so the total area used is about 8x5" (or 4*10").
