#+TITLE: Tube Blocks

[[./tube-blocks.png]]

Holds a specific diameter of tubing at the correct orientation in the bridgeport for mitering, or on the surface plate.

* Sizes

- 3/4" (round chainstays; holding hole saw arbors for removal)
  - 1-1/2" SQ, 2" long
- 1" (traditional tt)
  - +1-3/4" SQ, 2" long+
  - +2" SQ, 2" long+
  - 2-1/4" SQ, 2-1/4" long (not offered by Paragon, but consistent with others)
- 1-1/8" (tt/dt/st)
  - +1-3/4" SQ, 2" long+
  - +2" SQ, 2" long+
  - 2-1/4" SQ, 2-1/4" long
- 1-1/4" (tt/dt/st)
  - +2" SQ, 2" long+
  - 2-1/4" SQ, 2-1/4" long
- 1-3/8" (tt/dt/st)
  - +2" SQ, 2" long+
  - 2-1/4" SQ, 2-1/4" long
- 1-1/2" (dt)
  - 2-1/4" SQ, 2-1/4" long

* Materials

6061 aluminum, 2-1/2" square extruded bar, 6 blocks 2-1/2" long (accounts for kerf)
6*2.5 = 15"
